Casino Royale : The greatest bond film

Casino Royale was released in November of 2006. It stars Daniel Craig and Eva Green. The film revolves around James Bond battling a French financier and math genius by the name of Le Chiffre. The film grossed 599 million dollars on a budget of 150 million dollars.

This was Daniel Craig's first outing as James Bond

He was very successful in following his predecesor Pierce Brosnan.
